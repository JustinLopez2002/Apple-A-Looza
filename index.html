Apple-A-Looza

Fun and Fresh!

Event Details

Apple-A-Looza is Mississauga's celebration of fruits and vegetables. Come down to Gathering Central this weekend to spend the day with fellow enthusiasts of all things garden-grown! Are you a gardener? An aspiring chef? Or just interested in vegetation? Apple-A-Looza is the event for you!

No tickets needed! No entry fee required!

When and Where?

April 5 - 7

Friday, Saturday, and Sunday!

Gathering Central

123, City Street, Mississauga, Ontario
